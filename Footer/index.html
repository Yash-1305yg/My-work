About Project

Over 1.3 billion people live in India, making it the 2nd most populous country in the world. From the vast deserts of Rajasthan and the blissful beaches of Goa to the vibrant Kerala tea plantations and the busy metropolitan cities of Mumbai and Kolkata, India has huge diversity of landscapes, religion and culture. All of us get confused while choosing a travel destination for vacation so we have created a one stop destination where one can find the best tourist location of every state by browsing photos, videos, and blogs. From sky touching mountains and valleys of Kashmir to beautiful temples of Kanyakumari one can find every location on our website.
